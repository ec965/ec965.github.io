The C-ing Hat

Hack UCI 2019

DevPost GitHub

This hackathon project is a hat that can detect objects up to 1 meter in front, left and right of the wearer. It then sounds out a buzzer sound with pitch based on how close the object is to the person. The farther away the object is, the lower in pitch the sound is. The hat also makes longer buzzer sounds if the object is farther away. There are LEDs on the side of the hat to alert people around the wearer.
