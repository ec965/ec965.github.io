Coffee Grams

March 2020 - Current

GitHub

I started getting into making good coffee. The way to make a good cup of coffee every single time is to record the weight of the coffee and water used. I decided to create a scale that could record my cofee to water weight ratio into a database. I'm also planning to build out a front end to visualize the collected data. The first prototype used a Raspberry Pi. I'm considering switching to using an ESP32 and I've designed a PCB for this set up.
